Privacy Policy

Void Hopper

Overview

Void Hopper is a mobile game developed by Kevin Delao. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Void Hopper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

Local Storage

The app stores game progress (high scores, unlocked items, and preferences) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics and Tracking

Void Hopper does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. No usage data is collected or shared.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact
