## Full Description (Both Stores)
Launch into the void and see how far you can climb!

Void Hopper is a fast-paced vertical action game where you bounce between walls, dodge deadly enemies, and collect coins to unlock new bird skins and trails.

FEATURES:
- One-touch controls — tap to launch, tilt to steer
- 15+ unlockable bird skins including Parrot, Eagle, Owl, Phoenix and more
- Premium animated skins with shimmer, fire, electric, and cosmic effects
- Dynamic mood system — play well to power up your bird
- Procedurally generated levels that get harder as you climb
- Guardian boss encounters with unique attack patterns
- Multiple difficulty modes: Easy, Medium, and Hard
- Biome system with changing environments as you ascend
- Original procedural soundtrack that adapts to gameplay
- Near-miss bonus system rewards skilled play
- Wall bounce combos for extra points

How high can you climb?
